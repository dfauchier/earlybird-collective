Startups move fast

Earlybird is built for startups between 0-5 years old. We support company building through it's most volatile years. We recognise that what you need now and six months from now are likely different things. As temporary members of the team, we're used to picking up where we're needed and handing off when we're not.

Agility is everything

Working with us means embracing a more agile form of company building. One where we come in, raise the bar, order your chaos, and teach what we know until you outgrow us.

Freelancers, not consultants

We prefer doing to advising. All of us come from a startup background, and we like to get our hands dirty. Each of us has a core speciality, but we're used to working across all disciplines.
