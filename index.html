Startups move fast

Needs change quickly, and requirements evolve. Oftentimes, hiring can't keep up, and there are strains between a team's vision and their ability to execute on it.

Earlybird is built to fill this awkward early gap

We support company building through it's most volatile years. We recognise that what you need now and six months from now are likely different things. As temporary members of the team, we're used to picking up where we're needed and handing off when we're not.

Agility is everything

Working with us means embracing a more agile form of company building. One where we come in, raise the bar, order your chaos, and teach what we know until you outgrow us.

Freelancers, not consultants

We prefer doing to advising. All of us come from a startup background, so we're jacks of all trades, and we like to get our hands dirty. On top of that, each of us has a core speciality, and that's what we're primarily here to help you with.
